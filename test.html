About Us

Welcome to MCET

Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology (MCET), one of the best engineering colleges in Coimbatore district, is a self-financing educational institution situated in Pollachi.

Dr. MCET is the vision of Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam, whose determination and dynamism made possible the realization of this institution of excellence. The college was established in 1998 to commemorate the 75th birthday of this great visionary Arutchelvar Dr. N. Mahalingam.

Dr. MCET – Top Ranked Engineering College in Coimbatore

Dr. Mahalingam College of Engineering and Technology (Dr. MCET) is a premier and popular Engineering College in Pollachi, Coimbatore renowned for its academic excellence. Established with the goal to empower students through knowledge, innovation and research, Dr. MCET offers an exceptional educational experience. Accredited by NAAC and approved by AICTE, Dr. MCET focuses on holistic development, fostering creativity, critical thinking and leadership. Our mission is to bridge the gaps in the industry-academia, while nurturing creativity and technical expertise. With a legacy of high-quality education, Dr. MCET remains a leader of innovative education in India.

Campus Facilities and Infrastructure

The Dr. MCET campus consists of an exceptional infrastructure, including smart classrooms with interactive tools, advanced laboratories, and educational workshops. Students access a well-stocked library with both physical resources like research journals, and virtual resources like e-books. The campus also features modern hostels, sports centres, and cafeterias, in order to ensure a convenient environment for students. Our innovation hubs and incubation centers encourage students to explore entrepreneurial ventures, while high-speed internet and tech enabled spaces support academic growth. Our facilities at Dr. MCET not only provide a learning environment, but also fosters creativity, collaboration and holistic development.

Career Development & Placement Opportunities at Coimbatore’s Top Engineering College

Dr. MCET’s placement cell has a remarkable success record, with our graduates securing roles with top recruiters like TCS, Infosys, and L&T. Our 85% placement record reflects our commitment to career support, including internship opportunities, workshops, and mock interviews. A lot of Dr. MCET alumni excel globally in various sectors like IT, engineering, and entrepreneurship. To make sure that our students are industry-ready, we provide regular campus drives, career counseling, and soft-skill programs, while collaborating with multinational organizations enables us to provide real-world exposure to our students.

Student Life and Extracurricular Activities

Student life at Dr. MCET thrives with cultural events, tech fests and various clubs like robotics, literacy, and sports. To build teamwork and confidence for our students, Dr. MCET offers various programs such as Annual Hackathons, TEDx Talks, and other leadership programs. The campus thrives with activities such as sports tournaments, eco-drives, and international exchange programs, fostering well-grounded growth. Also, students get to engage in community service, and innovation through NCC, NSS and entrepreneurship cells

Admission Process and Eligibility

Dr. MCET admission for B. Tech requires TNEA/JEE Main scores, while M.Tech/MBA admissions require GATE/TANCET. Eligibility for candidates include 10+2 (50% aggregate) for UG and relevant bachelor’s degrees for pursuing PG. Students can apply online through our website portal, attend counselling, and explore scholarships for merit and economically disadvantaged students. Our process is completely transparent and provides dedicated guidance to ensure easy and seamless admission enrollment.
